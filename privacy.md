- email address and other contact details you provide when contacting support;
- support communications;
- information you choose to provide in support requests.

### 3.2 Subscription and transaction data

- subscription status;
- plan type;
- purchase identifiers;
- renewal, cancellation, and trial events;
- app store transaction information;
- approximate country or storefront information relevant to billing.

Billing, payment processing, subscription management, refunds, and certain transaction records are handled by Apple App Store or Google Play, subject to their terms and privacy practices. We do not receive your full payment card details when purchases are processed through those platforms.

- app opens and sessions;

### 3.4 Device and technical data

- device model;

### 3.5 Advertising data

ShakeChef AI shows ads inside the app and runs advertising campaigns on third-party platforms. In connection with advertising, the following data may be processed:

- mobile advertising identifiers (e.g. Apple IDFA, Google Advertising ID / App Set ID) where permitted by your device settings and applicable consent;
- ad impression, view, click, and interaction events;
- ad unit and placement identifiers;
- approximate location derived from IP address or store country;
- coarse device, app, and session signals used for ad delivery, frequency capping, fraud prevention, and measurement;
- conversion and attribution signals, including first-open, install, subscription, and other in-app event data shared with advertising and attribution providers to measure and optimize our campaigns.

Whether and how personalized (interest-based) advertising is used depends on your device settings (for example Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt) and, where required, on your consent. If you do not consent to personalized advertising or tracking, you may still see ads, but they will not be personalized based on tracking across other apps and websites.

### 3.6 Data processed locally on your device only

Certain data may be processed locally on your device for app functionality and may not be transferred to our servers or shared by us with third parties. Depending on device support, permissions, and your settings, this may include:

- shake / motion sensor data used to detect when you shake your phone;
- certain personalization inputs used only for on-device functionality.

Where a feature uses such local-only data, our intention is that this data remains on your device unless we explicitly tell you otherwise in the app or in this Privacy Policy.

## 9. Your choices regarding advertising

You have several ways to control how your data is used for advertising:

- **iOS:** You can allow or deny tracking in the App Tracking Transparency prompt and change your choice at any time under *Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking*. You can also enable *Limit Ad Tracking* equivalents via *Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising*.
- **Android:** You can reset or delete your Advertising ID, or opt out of personalized advertising, under *Settings → Google → Ads*.
- **In-app consent:** Where we request consent for personalized ads or tracking, you can accept, decline, or change your choice using the in-app consent controls.
- **Industry opt-outs:** You can also use industry opt-out tools such as the [YourAdChoices](https://youradchoices.com/) / [EDAA](https://www.youronlinechoices.eu/) tools and the Google Ads Settings at [adssettings.google.com](https://adssettings.google.com/).

Opting out of personalized advertising does not stop ads from appearing; it only limits the use of your data for personalization and cross-app/-site tracking.
