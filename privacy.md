#### General
We take the protection of your personal data very seriously and process it exclusively in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.
This privacy policy informs you about the data we collect, the purposes for which we use it, and how we protect it.

#### 1. What data do we collect?

##### a) Email address
- If you sign up for our newsletter, we store your email address. It is used exclusively to inform you about news and offers from ShakeChef AI.

##### b) Technical browser data
- When you visit our website, we automatically store certain technical information, such as:
  - Your IP address (anonymized)
  - Date and time of access
  - Browser type and version
  - Operating system used
  - Referrer URL (the previously visited page)
This data is required to ensure the security and functionality of our website.

##### c) Hosting data
Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. As a result, when you visit our site, data may also be processed by GitHub (GitHub, Inc.). You can find GitHub's privacy policy at: [https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement).

#### 2. Purpose of data collection
- Email addresses: sending newsletters and information.
- Browser data: ensuring technical operation and analyzing website usage.

#### 3. Legal basis
Your data is processed in accordance with Art. 6 (1) GDPR:
- For the newsletter, based on your consent (Art. 6 (1) lit. a GDPR).
- For technical browser data, based on our legitimate interest in providing and securing the website (Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR).

#### 4. Storage and deletion of data
- Newsletter data: Your email address is stored until you unsubscribe from the newsletter. After unsubscribing, the email address will be deleted.
- Browser data: see point 1c.

#### 5. Data sharing
Your data is generally not passed on to third parties. An exception is the processing by our hosting provider GitHub (see point 1c).

#### 6. Use of third-party services

##### a) Google Forms and Google Sheets
- We use Google Forms and Google Sheets for our surveys and data collection.
- When participating in our surveys, the following data is collected:
  - Email address (if provided in the form)
  - Answers to the survey questions
  - Timestamp of survey participation

###### Data protection with Google Forms and Google Sheets:
- The collected data is stored in Google Sheets and processed by Google in accordance with their privacy policy.
- We do not have access to any additional personal data beyond the information you have provided.
- Google provides security measures to protect your data, including encryption and access controls.

###### Your rights:
- You can withdraw your consent to data processing at any time.
- You have the right to access, rectify, delete, and restrict the processing of your data.
- Contact us at hi@mietzekater.de for further information or requests.

##### b) Web analytics with counter.dev
We use counter.dev as a very limited, privacy-friendly analytics tool to better understand traffic on our websites. Only anonymized and aggregated data is collected, such as page views and time spent on the site. No personal data is collected, and the analysis is used exclusively to improve the functionality and security of our website.
Processing is based on our legitimate interest in accordance with Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR. For more information on data protection at counter.dev, please see their privacy policy at: [https://counter.dev/pages/privacy.html](https://counter.dev/pages/privacy.html).

#### Meta (Facebook) advertising
We use Meta advertising services and analytics tools. The following data may be collected:
- Device information
- Usage behavior
- Advertising ID
- In-app activity

Further information can be found in the [Meta Privacy Policy](https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy).

#### Google Play Services
By using Google Play Services, various data is collected, including:
- Crash reports
- Usage statistics
- Device information
- Performance data

## 4. Legal bases

Data is processed on the basis of:
- Art. 6 (1) lit. a GDPR (consent)
- Art. 6 (1) lit. b GDPR (contract performance)
- Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR (legitimate interests)
